The Flow

From first hour to collected payment
in six steps

The entire billing lifecycle, automated at every stage that can be automated — and frictionless at every stage that can't.

1
Log Time
One tap to start a timer. Switch matters on the fly. Reconstruct at day's end if needed.
2
Pre-bill Review
Review entries before billing. Clean descriptions, apply write-offs, approve time.
3
Generate Invoice
Rates apply automatically. Branded PDF built in seconds, ready to review.
4
Send to Client
Email delivery with pay link, or export as LEDES for e-billing clients.
5
Collect Payment
Online payment, trust drawdown, or check — all posted automatically to the invoice.
6
Reconcile
Syncs to QuickBooks. Realization rate calculated. Reports updated in real time.
